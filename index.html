Welcome to Mel's Barre

You get a surprise for your first class!

Mel's Barre is a Barre fitness studio which provides online and in-person classes. (As we are still in times of COVID-19, we will be taking extra precaution in opening our studio, so call classes will be online.) Barre offers many benefits to both physical and mental health.

  • Barre is a mix of ballet and cardio exercises
  • Barre can uplift your mood and spirits
  • Barre can increase your flexibility and stamina
  • Barre can be done from anywhere
  • Barre is FUN!
