About Mel's Barre

Who is Mel?
Mel is the business owner of Mel's Barre. She was a dancer for 11 years before she had to take a step back due to an economic recession. After ten years of going on a dance hiatus, Mel decided to get back into fitness doing Barre, which she loves. At first, what started as a hobby, is now a side income for her, as she loves to spend time with her students.
When was Mel's Barre founded?
Mel's Barre was founded June 2020.
Do you do anything for charity?
Currently, we do not do anything for local charities, but plan to in the future. For those that have a low-income, we do try to accomodate in lower rates. These rates will not appear in our table, and will have to be discussed by phone or through the Contact/Sign-Up page.
